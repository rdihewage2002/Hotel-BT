About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

Founded in 2019, HBT has been a symbol of hospitality and elegance in Colombo for decades. Originally established as a charming boutique inn, our hotel has grown and evolved while preserving its historic charm and commitment to exceptional service. Over the years, we have welcomed countless guests, including notable figures, who have enjoyed the blend of classic architecture and modern amenities. Today, HBT continues to honor its rich heritage while offering a contemporary and luxurious experience for all who walk through our doors.
